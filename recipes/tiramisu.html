Tiramisu

You can make your very own Italian Authentic Grandma approved Tiramisu by following this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce of unsweetened cocoa (for sprinkling on the top layer)
  • 1/2 cup coffee flavoured liqueur (e.g Kahl√∫a)
  • 1 cup cold espresso or strong coffee
  • 30 italian ladyfingers
  • 1 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 1/4 cup mascarpone cheese at room temperature
  • 1 cup sugar (superfine recommended)
  • 6 large egg yolks

Steps

  1. Pre-sauce preparation

    Pour one inch of water in a small pot and bring to a boil. Once boiled set pot to simmer. Place bowl on top of small pot with out touching the water.

  2. Making the sabayon (sweet) sauce

    In the bowl add egg yolks and sugar. Use a whisk to mix them together for 10 min. Whip until mixture has a thick and lemon colour. Let it cool down.

  3. Adding mascarpone to sauce

    Add Mascarpone to the sabayon and mix well until combined.

  4. Whipped cream

    In a separate bowl, mix whip cream to stiff peaks.

  5. Adding whipped cream to mixture

    Gently fold the whipped cream into the mixture. Put in refrigerator until ready to use.

  6. Cold espresso and coffee liqueur

    Mix cold espresso and coffee liqueur to mixture.

  7. Ladyfingers

    Dip ladyfingers into mixture long enough to get them wet but not soaked.

  8. Layering - Bottom of Cake

    Place ladyfingers in the bottom of a 9-inch square baking dish.

  9. Layering - 3rd Layer of Cake

    Spoon half of mixture over the ladyfingers.

  10. Layering - 2nd Layer of Cake

    Add another layer of ladyfingers.

  11. Layering - 1st Layer of Cake

    Cover the ladyfingers with mixture.

  12. Refrigerator

    Put finished product in the fridge for at least 4 hours (Overnight recommended).

  13. Dusting

    Add cocoa powder to top layer of tiramisu.
